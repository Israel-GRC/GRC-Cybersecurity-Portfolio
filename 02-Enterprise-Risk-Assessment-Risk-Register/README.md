# Project 2 – Enterprise Risk Assessment & Risk Register

## Status
Completed

## Overview
This project demonstrates the enterprise risk management lifecycle for Acme Financial Services. The project includes identifying critical assets, assessing cybersecurity risks, documenting risk ratings, creating a risk register, developing a risk treatment plan, and preparing an executive risk summary.

## Deliverables

- Company Risk Profile
- Risk Assessment Methodology
- Enterprise Risk Assessment & Risk Register Workbook
- Risk Treatment Plan
- Executive Risk Summary

## Workbook Includes

- Asset Inventory
- Risk Register
- Risk Matrix
- Risk Heat Map
- Lists
- Definitions

## Skills Demonstrated

- Enterprise Risk Assessment
- Asset Inventory Management
- Risk Register Development
- Risk Scoring
- Risk Treatment Planning
- Executive Risk Reporting
- Cybersecurity Governance
- GRC Documentation

## Framework Alignment

This project was developed using concepts aligned with:

- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) 2.0
- NIST Risk Management concepts
- ISO/IEC 27001
- Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC) best practices
